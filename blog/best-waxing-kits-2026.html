— Tested & Ranked

We tested four of the top-rated kits across all categories to find the best option for every budget and hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between hard wax and soft wax?

Hard wax (like Cirepil Blue) hardens on skin and is removed without strips. It's gentler, ideal for sensitive areas, and grips coarse hair better. Soft wax (like GiGi Honee) is applied thin and removed with muslin strips — faster for legs and arms. Most at-home users find hard wax more forgiving.

How do I avoid ingrown hairs after waxing?

Exfoliate 24 hours before waxing to lift trapped hairs. Starting 2–3 days after waxing, exfoliate gently 2–3 times per week. Salicylic acid or glycolic acid products help keep follicle openings clear. Avoid tight clothing and heat (hot showers, sun, chlorine) for 24 hours post-wax.

How long does hair need to be to wax?

For soft wax: at least 1/4 inch (6mm) — about 2 weeks of post-shave growth. For hard wax: as short as 1/8 inch (3mm). Hair over 1/2 inch should be trimmed first to avoid unnecessary pain. The ideal length is 1/4 to 1/3 inch for most wax types.
