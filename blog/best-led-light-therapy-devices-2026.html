Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use LED light therapy?

Most devices recommend daily use for 4–8 weeks for initial results, then 2–3 times per week for maintenance. Consistency matters more than intensity — even 10 minutes daily beats 30-minute weekly sessions.

What wavelength is best for anti-aging?

633nm red light and 830nm near-infrared have the strongest clinical evidence for stimulating collagen and reducing fine lines. Look for masks that specifically list these wavelengths rather than just saying "red LED."

Are at-home LED devices as effective as in-office treatments?

In-office devices are more powerful, but at-home devices compensate with frequency. Daily 10-minute sessions over 8 weeks have shown comparable results to bi-weekly in-office treatments in several studies.
