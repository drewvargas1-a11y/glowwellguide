Frequently Asked Questions

Rotary vs. foil shaver — which is better?

Foil shavers (Braun) deliver a closer shave and work best if you shave daily. Rotary shavers (Philips) flex to follow facial contours and handle longer stubble better — ideal if you shave every 2–3 days or have coarser hair. For most people who shave daily, foil wins on closeness; rotary wins on comfort with longer growth.

Is wet shaving with an electric razor actually better?

For sensitive skin, yes — significantly. Using shaving gel or foam with a waterproof electric shaver (Braun Series 9 Pro+ and Philips Norelco 9500 are both IPX7) reduces friction and virtually eliminates irritation. The closeness isn't dramatically different, but comfort improves noticeably. If you get razor burn from dry electric shaving, switch to wet — it's a game changer.

How often should you replace electric shaver foils?

Every 12–18 months for daily use. Signs it's time: increased pulling, more skin irritation, or noticeably less closeness. Replacement heads for Braun and Philips run $30–$60 but restore near-new performance. Skipping foil replacement is the #1 reason people think their electric shaver has worn out when it just needs new blades.
