It uses the Coanda effect — a physics phenomenon where high-speed air jets cause surrounding air to spiral, which naturally attracts and wraps hair strands around the barrel without clips or extreme heat. The Complete Long kit includes 6 attachments covering smoothing brushes, soft curl barrels, and diffuser, making it a single tool that replaces an entire styling drawer. Frequently Asked Questions

Does a hair dryer brush damage hair more than a regular dryer?

Not necessarily. It depends on the device and technique. Premium devices like the Dyson Airwrap use controlled low-temperature airflow well below the damage threshold. For budget options like the Revlon: always use heat protectant, keep the brush moving, and start with the lowest effective heat setting. The key risk is holding one section stationary with high heat for too long.

What size barrel is best for a hair dryer brush?

For volume and body: 2–2.5 inch oval barrel (the Revlon standard). For large waves: 2 inch round. For tighter definition: 1.5 inch or smaller. For fine hair: smaller barrels add definition; for thick/long hair, larger barrels work faster. The oval barrel on the Revlon hits the sweet spot for most users.

Is a hair dryer brush worth it vs. a regular dryer?

For people who blow-dry regularly, yes. A dryer brush saves 40–60% of styling time and produces smoother, more voluminous results than a dryer alone for most hair types. The $45 Revlon investment pays for itself quickly in time savings versus the two-tool approach.
