Frequently Asked Questions

Does at-home IPL hair removal actually work?

Yes — with realistic expectations and consistency. After 8–12 weekly sessions, most users with light-to-medium skin and dark hair see 70–90% hair reduction lasting months to years. Results aren't as fast as professional laser, but the best devices (Ulike Air 3, Braun Silk-Expert Pro 5) produce genuinely significant reduction. IPL works best on dark hair — it's less effective on blonde, red, or gray hair, and not safe for dark skin tones (Fitzpatrick V–VI).

How many treatments before you see results?

Most users see noticeable thinning after 4–6 weekly sessions. Full results — 70–90% reduction — typically take 8–12 treatments. Hair grows in cycles, so you need to treat during the active growth phase multiple times. After initial treatment, monthly maintenance sessions keep hair from returning. Expect hair to shed 1–2 weeks after each treatment, not immediately.

Is at-home IPL safe for all skin tones?

No — standard IPL is not safe for dark skin tones (Fitzpatrick V–VI). On darker skin, IPL can target melanin in the skin itself rather than only the follicle, causing burns or hyperpigmentation. The Braun Silk-Expert Pro 5 has the best built-in skin sensor that automatically blocks the flash if your tone is unsafe. Always patch test 24 hours before full treatment. People with Fitzpatrick V–VI skin should use professional Nd:YAG laser treatments instead.
