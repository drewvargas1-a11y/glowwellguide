Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Dyson Airwrap worth the $599 price?

For frequent stylists, yes — the reduced heat damage adds up over time. If you style daily, styling at 150°C instead of 230°C genuinely makes a difference to hair health after months. For occasional stylists, the GHD Platinum+ or Shark FlexStyle gives better value per use. The Airwrap earns its price through daily use — not as a luxury purchase that sits in a drawer.

What heat setting should I use on a flat iron?

Fine or color-treated hair: 300–350°F. Normal hair: 350–400°F. Thick, coarse, or curly hair: 400–450°F. The GHD Platinum+ locks to 365°F — the sweet spot that minimizes damage while straightening efficiently. Never exceed 450°F regardless of hair type; above that you're burning the cuticle, not styling it.

How do I make curls last longer?

Three keys: (1) Let each curl cool in your hand before releasing — this sets the shape. (2) Use a light-hold spray before curling, not after. (3) Smaller barrels create tighter curls that hold longer. The T3 CurlWrap's auto-draw also helps by maintaining consistent tension on every strand, which produces curls that hold more evenly throughout the day.
