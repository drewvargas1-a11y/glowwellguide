Frequently Asked Questions

Does microcurrent actually work for lifting and toning?

Yes — with realistic expectations. Microcurrent stimulates facial muscles with low-level electrical current, similar to physical therapy. Clinical studies show measurable improvement in contour and firmness with consistent use (5x per week for 60 days, then 2–3x maintenance). Results are subtle and cumulative. Think of it like exercise: you won't see dramatic change after one session, but at 60 days of consistency, the lift and definition are visible.

How long until you see results from microcurrent?

Initial results (improved radiance, slight lift) typically appear within 2–4 weeks of daily use. Significant, lasting changes to facial contour appear at 60 days with consistent use. NuFace's clinical data shows 85% of users reporting visible improvement in facial contour after 60 days. Consistency is everything — sporadic use won't produce results.

Can I use microcurrent if I have fillers or Botox?

For metal implants in the face or neck: do not use microcurrent. For dermal fillers: most dermatologists recommend waiting 2 weeks post-injection and avoiding direct use over filler sites. For Botox: wait 2 weeks. Always consult your provider if you're unsure — NuFace and Foreo both include detailed contraindication lists in their packaging.
