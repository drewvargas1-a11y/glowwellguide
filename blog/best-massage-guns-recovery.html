Frequently Asked Questions

How long should I use a massage gun per muscle group?

1–2 minutes per muscle group is the evidence-backed recommendation. More is not better — extended use can cause bruising or aggravate existing inflammation. Use it before workouts for activation (30–60 sec) and after for recovery (1–2 min).

What does amplitude actually mean?

Amplitude is how far the head travels per stroke (in millimeters). Higher amplitude = deeper tissue penetration. The Theragun Elite's 16mm reaches deep muscle tissue; 10mm is surface-level relief. For chronic tightness and serious recovery, prioritize amplitude over speed (PPM).

Where should you NOT use a massage gun?

Never use directly on joints, bones, the spine, or recently injured tissue. Avoid the neck (arteries and nerves run close to the surface). If you have a specific injury, check with a physical therapist before using percussive therapy on that area.
