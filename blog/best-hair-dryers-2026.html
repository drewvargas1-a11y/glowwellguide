Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Dyson Supersonic worth $429?

For fine, damaged, or color-treated hair — yes. The heat control alone prevents the damage that cheaper dryers cause over time. If you have thick, healthy hair and just need to dry quickly, the Shark FlexStyle or BaBylissPRO deliver 80% of the result at half the price.

What wattage should I look for?

1875W is the minimum we'd recommend. 2000W+ is ideal for thick or long hair. The Dyson is an exception — its high-speed motor compensates for lower wattage with superior airflow technology.

How do I prevent heat damage while blow drying?

Always use a heat protectant spray first. Keep the dryer 6 inches from your hair and keep it moving. Use the cool shot button to set your style at the end. Avoid the highest heat setting for daily use.
