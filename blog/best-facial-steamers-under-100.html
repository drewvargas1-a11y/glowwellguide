Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I steam my face?

1–2 times per week is the sweet spot for most skin types. More than 3x/week can strip natural oils and cause dryness. If you have oily skin, you can steam up to 3 times weekly. Dry or sensitive skin: stick to once a week.

Do I use steamer before or after cleanser?

After cleanser, before serums and moisturizer. Clean skin + open pores = maximum absorption of whatever you apply next. Never steam over makeup.

Can I add essential oils to my steamer?

Only if the steamer is specifically designed for it (most are not). Adding oils to a standard steamer can damage the heating element and void your warranty. Use distilled water only unless the manufacturer says otherwise.
