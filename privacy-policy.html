Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Overview

LumaGrid Reviews ("we," "us," or "our") operates lumagridreviews.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site. We believe in keeping it simple: we collect very little data, we don't sell it, and we don't share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

We collect a limited amount of data to understand how our site is used and to improve our content:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time on page, browser type, and device type, collected anonymously through analytics tools
  • Email addresses — only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter or contact us directly
  • Cookies — used to understand traffic patterns and improve the site experience

We do not collect names, physical addresses, payment information, or any sensitive personal data.

Amazon Associates & Affiliate Links

LumaGrid Reviews participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click an affiliate link on our site and make a purchase, Amazon may set cookies to track that referral. Amazon's privacy practices govern how that data is collected and used — see Amazon's Privacy Notice. We also participate in affiliate programs through Commission Junction and Impact, which may set similar tracking cookies.

Analytics

We use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect anonymized usage data. This helps us understand which guides are most useful and how to improve the site. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Understand how visitors use our site and which content is most helpful
  • Improve the quality and relevance of our buying guides
  • Respond to emails and questions you send us
  • Send newsletters to subscribers who have opted in

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Links

Our site links to external retailers, brand websites, and other resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any site you visit through a link on ours.

Children's Privacy

LumaGrid Reviews is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make a request, email us at hello@lumagridreviews.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
