Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: May 28, 2026

LumaGrid Reviews ("we," "us," or "our") is a participant in several affiliate advertising programs designed to provide a means for sites to earn commissions by linking to products. This page explains how that works and how it affects (and doesn't affect) the content you read on this site.

Amazon Associates Program

LumaGrid Reviews is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com. Our affiliate tag is geargridrevie-20.

When you click an Amazon link on our site and make a qualifying purchase, we earn a small commission — at no additional cost to you. The price you pay is the same whether you use our link or go directly to Amazon.

Other Affiliate Programs

In addition to Amazon Associates, we participate in affiliate programs through Commission Junction (CJ) and Impact. These programs connect us with brands in the health, beauty, and wellness space. The same principles apply: clicking our links may result in a commission if you make a purchase.

How This Affects Our Content

It doesn't. Our editorial rankings, ratings, and recommendations are based entirely on research, expert consensus, and real user validation — not on which products pay higher commissions. We follow the same methodology for every guide: read how we test →

  • We do not accept payment for positive reviews or rankings
  • We do not allow brands to review or influence our editorial content before publication
  • We call out product flaws and limitations as clearly as we highlight strengths
  • If we think a cheaper alternative performs just as well, we say so — even if the expensive option pays a higher commission

FTC Compliance

In accordance with the FTC's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255), we disclose our affiliate relationships on every page that contains affiliate links, including a prominent disclosure bar at the top of each page.

Questions?

If you have any questions about how our affiliate program works or how we select products for our guides, please contact us and we'll be happy to explain.
